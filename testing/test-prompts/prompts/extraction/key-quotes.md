# Key Quotes Extraction

**Tests:** Salience detection, quotable moment identification, attribution accuracy

---

Listen to this audio recording and extract the most significant, quotable, or memorable statements.

## Extraction Criteria

### What Makes a Quote "Key"
- **Impactful statements**: Strong opinions, definitive claims
- **Summary moments**: Statements that encapsulate main points
- **Memorable phrasing**: Well-articulated or distinctive expressions
- **Decision points**: Moments where conclusions are reached
- **Emotional peaks**: Statements with strong sentiment
- **Surprising revelations**: Unexpected information or admissions

### Output Format

```
KEY QUOTES:

1. "[Exact quote]"
   - Speaker: [Attribution]
   - Context: [What prompted this statement]
   - Significance: [Why this quote matters]

2. "[Quote]"
   - Speaker: [Attribution]
   - Context: [Context]
   - Significance: [Why notable]

...

MOST QUOTABLE:
"[Single best quote from the recording]"

```

## Guidelines

1. **Verbatim accuracy**: Quotes must be exact, including any grammatical quirks.

2. **Fair representation**: Don't cherry-pick quotes that misrepresent the speaker's overall message.

3. **Context is key**: Always provide enough context to understand the quote.

4. **Quantity**: Aim for 5-10 key quotes depending on recording length. Quality over quantity.

5. **Attribution confidence**: If uncertain who said something, indicate this.

The result should capture the most impactful moments from the conversation in quotable form.
