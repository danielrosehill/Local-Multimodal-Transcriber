# Executive Summary

**Tests:** Concise summarization, key point extraction, professional synthesis

---

Listen to this audio recording and produce an executive summary suitable for busy stakeholders.

## Summary Requirements

### Structure

```
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Overview: [1-2 sentences capturing the essence]

Key Points:
- [Point 1]
- [Point 2]
- [Point 3]

Decisions Made:
- [Decision 1]
- [Decision 2]

Action Items:
- [Action 1] - Owner: [Name], Due: [Date]
- [Action 2] - Owner: [Name], Due: [Date]

Open Questions:
- [Question 1]

Recommendation: [If applicable, one sentence recommendation]
```

### Guidelines

1. **Brevity**: Maximum 200 words for the entire summary
2. **Objectivity**: Report facts, not interpretations
3. **Prioritization**: Lead with the most important information
4. **Actionability**: Focus on what matters for decision-making
5. **No fluff**: Every word should earn its place

The result should allow someone to understand the essential content without listening to the recording.
