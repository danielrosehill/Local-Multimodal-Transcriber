# Voice Demographics and Characteristics

**Tests:** Age estimation, gender perception, physical characteristic inference, speaker profiling

---

Based solely on vocal characteristics, provide your analysis of the speaker's demographic profile. Be clear that these are inferences based on acoustic patterns, not certainties.

Analyze:

1. **Approximate age range**:
   - What age bracket does the voice suggest? (child, teen, young adult, middle-aged, elderly)
   - What specific vocal characteristics inform this estimate? (pitch range, vocal fry patterns, articulation clarity, vibrato characteristics)
   - Provide a range rather than a specific number.

2. **Vocal characteristics suggesting physical traits**:
   - Fundamental frequency range (typically correlates with vocal tract size)
   - Resonance characteristics
   - Breathiness, clarity, or other quality markers
