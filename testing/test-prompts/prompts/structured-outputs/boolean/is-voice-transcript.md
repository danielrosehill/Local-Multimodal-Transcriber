# Is This a Voice Transcript?

**Tests:** Audio type classification, speech vs. non-speech detection, recording format identification

**Response type:** Boolean (Yes/No)

---

Is this audio recording a voice transcript (human speech intended to be transcribed)?

Respond with only: **Yes** or **No**

Answer **Yes** if:
- The audio contains human speech as the primary content
- The speech appears intended for transcription or documentation
- The recording is a voice memo, dictation, or verbal communication

Answer **No** if:
- The audio is primarily music, sound effects, or environmental sounds
- The audio contains no discernible human speech
- The speech is incidental background noise rather than the main content
- The audio is a song, podcast intro music, or non-speech audio content
