# Is This a Dictated Email?

**Tests:** Content classification, communication format detection, email structure recognition

**Response type:** Boolean (Yes/No)

---

Is this audio message a dictated email?

Respond with only: **Yes** or **No**

Answer **Yes** if:
- The speaker is clearly dictating an email to be sent
- The content includes email-like elements: greeting, body message, sign-off
- The speaker mentions a recipient, subject line, or explicitly indicates they are composing an email
- The structure and tone suggest written correspondence intended for email delivery

Answer **No** if:
- The recording is a general voice memo or note to self
- The content is a conversation, meeting, or discussion
- The speaker is dictating other document types (letters, reports, articles)
- The recording is a list, reminder, or task-oriented message
- There are no indicators that the content is intended to be sent as an email
