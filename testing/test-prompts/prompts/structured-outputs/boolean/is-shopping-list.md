# Is This a Shopping List?

**Tests:** Content classification, list detection, purchase intent recognition

**Response type:** Boolean (Yes/No)

---

Is this audio message a shopping list?

Respond with only: **Yes** or **No**

Answer **Yes** if:
- The recording primarily consists of items the speaker intends to purchase
- The content is structured as a list of things to buy
- The speaker is dictating items for shopping (any type of shopping: groceries, household items, electronics, clothing, etc.)

Answer **No** if:
- The recording is a general conversation that happens to mention products
- The content is primarily narrative or conversational, not list-based
- Items mentioned are not intended for purchase
- The recording is a to-do list, inventory, or other non-shopping list type
- The speaker is describing what they already bought (past tense) rather than what they need to buy
