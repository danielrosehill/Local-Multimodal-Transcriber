# Is This a Grocery List?

**Tests:** Content classification, food/household item detection, specific list type identification

**Response type:** Boolean (Yes/No)

---

Is this audio message a grocery list?

Respond with only: **Yes** or **No**

Answer **Yes** if:
- The recording primarily consists of food items or household consumables to purchase
- The content is a list of groceries (food, beverages, cleaning supplies, toiletries, etc.)
- The speaker is dictating items typically purchased at a grocery store or supermarket

Answer **No** if:
- The shopping list is for non-grocery items (electronics, clothing, furniture, etc.)
- The recording discusses food but is not a shopping list (recipes, meal plans, food reviews)
- The content is primarily narrative rather than list-based
- The speaker mentions grocery items incidentally within a broader conversation
- The recording is a restaurant order or meal request rather than a shopping list
