# Transcribe as Email

**Tests:** Format transformation, spoken-to-written conversion, professional communication adaptation, structural reorganization

---

Listen to this audio recording and convert its content into a well-formatted email.

Transform the spoken content into email format:

1. **Subject line**: Create an appropriate subject line that captures the main topic or purpose.

2. **Greeting**: Add an appropriate salutation based on context (or use a placeholder if the recipient is unclear).

3. **Body**: Convert the spoken content into clear, professional email prose:
   - Organize information logically with paragraphs
   - Use bullet points or numbered lists where appropriate
   - Maintain all key information and requests
   - Adapt casual speech to appropriate written tone

4. **Action items**: If the speaker mentions tasks, requests, or next steps, make these clearly visible.

5. **Closing**: Add an appropriate sign-off.

Preserve:
- All substantive information and requests
- Specific names, dates, numbers, and details
- The speaker's intent and priorities

Adapt:
- Spoken disfluencies to written clarity
- Rambling structure to logical organization
- Casual tone to professional (unless context suggests otherwise)
