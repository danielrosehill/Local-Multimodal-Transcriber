## Meeting Minutes Structure

1. **Header**:
   - Meeting title/purpose
   - Date and time (if mentioned, or use placeholder)
   - Attendees (list all participants mentioned)
   - Location/format (in-person, virtual, etc.)

2. **Agenda items discussed**: For each topic covered:
   - Topic heading
   - Key discussion points (bullet format)
   - Decisions made
   - Dissenting views or concerns raised (if any)

3. **Action items**: Clearly formatted table or list with:
   - Task description
   - Owner/responsible party
   - Due date (if mentioned)
   - Priority (if indicated)

4. **Decisions log**: Summary of all decisions reached:
   - What was decided
   - Rationale (if discussed)
   - Any conditions or dependencies

5. **Parking lot**: Issues raised but deferred for later discussion.

6. **Next steps**:
   - Follow-up meeting date (if scheduled)
   - Items for next agenda
   - Outstanding questions

Preserve:
- All decisions and their context
- Specific commitments and assignments
- Names associated with responsibilities
- Exact figures, dates, and deadlines mentioned

Adapt:
- Conversational discussion to structured summaries
- Verbal back-and-forth to consolidated points
- Informal agreements to formal documented decisions

The result should be suitable for distribution to attendees and stakeholders.
