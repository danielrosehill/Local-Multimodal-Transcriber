## Note types (adapt format accordingly):

- **Idea capture**: Focus on the core insight; add "explore further" notes
- **Reminder**: Emphasize the trigger (when/what prompts action)
- **Reference**: Organize for quick lookup
- **Decision made**: Document the decision and key reasoning
- **Conversation summary**: Who, what was discussed, what's next

Preserve:
- The essential information you'd be frustrated to lose
- Specific details (names, numbers, dates)
- The speaker's original intent and urgency

Adapt:
- Verbose thinking-out-loud to concise points
- Repeated self-reminders to single clear note
- Scattered thoughts to organized quick-reference

The result should be useful when rediscovered days or weeks later.
